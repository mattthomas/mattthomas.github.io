Matt Thomas

I’m a teacher and writer currently living in Iowa City, IA. Primarily, I’m interested in the relationship between media, habits, identity, and culture in the United States. Sometimes I design, build, and maintain websites. Before all this, I used to do other stuff, including work in film production and private security. I’m originally from Seattle. I have a B.A. from the University of Southern California and a Ph.D. from the University of Iowa. Both in American Studies. My dissertation project was a critical history of the life hacking movement.

For more, check out my blog, follow me on Twitter, and/or send me an email.
